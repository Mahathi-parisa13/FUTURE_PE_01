UGC Ad Prompt System

AI-Powered UGC Ad Script Generator for Local Businesses and Brands

1. Introduction

Today’s brands grow faster with UGC-style ads that feel real, honest, and relatable. These ads perform better because they sound like normal customer experiences instead of traditional promotions.

2. Objective

  • Generate short UGC-style ad scripts
  • Create multiple hooks for one product or business
  • Follow problem → solution → CTA structure
  • Adapt tone for different platforms
  • Create ad-ready content for Reels, Shorts, and Ads

3. Business Chosen

Glow & Grace Beauty Salon – Vijayawada

This local salon was chosen as a real example to create UGC ad scripts that feel natural, relatable, and ready for social media promotions.

4. Tools Used

  • ChatGPT
  • Claude
  • Google Gemini
  • CapCut
  • Runway

5. Sample Hooks

1. I finally found a salon I actually trust.

2. My hair has never looked this good.

3. Vijayawada girls, you need to try this salon.

4. I booked one appointment… now I keep going back.

5. Why is nobody talking about this salon?

6. UGC Ad Script

“Okay, I need to tell you about this salon. I’ve tried so many salons before, but every time, my haircut never looked the way I wanted. Then one of my friends suggested Glow & Grace Beauty Salon in Vijayawada. The staff actually listened to what I wanted, and the result was honestly so good. If you’re looking for a new salon in Vijayawada, just book an appointment here.”

7. Platform-Specific Style

Platform Style
Instagram Reels Fast, emotional, relatable
YouTube Shorts Story-based, slightly detailed
Facebook Ads Clear, direct, trust-focused
TikTok Casual, quick, fun

8. Conclusion

This project shows how AI and Prompt Engineering can help businesses create high-converting UGC ad content. It can be used as a portfolio project, freelance service, or agency offering.
