AI Prompt Framework for Local Business Website Copy

Conversion-Focused Website Copy System for a Local Business Using Prompt Engineering + AI

1. Objective

The purpose of this project is to build a reusable prompt system that generates high-quality website copy for local businesses.

This system helps create:

  • Strong homepage copy
  • Clear service descriptions
  • Persuasive call-to-action sections
  • Tone-adjusted content for different business types
  • Website copy that is ready to publish

2. Business Chosen

Glow & Grace Beauty Salon

Location: Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh

Business Type: Salon and Beauty Services

Problem Faced by the Business:

  • Generic website text
  • Weak value proposition
  • No clear call-to-action
  • Services not properly described
  • Visitors may leave without booking

Goal: Create website copy that builds trust, explains the salon’s value, and encourages appointment bookings.

3. Tools Used

  • ChatGPT
  • Claude
  • Google Gemini
  • Lovable
  • Framer AI

4. Reusable Master Prompt

You are an expert website copywriter for local businesses. Create conversion-focused website copy for: Business Name: [BUSINESS NAME] Business Type: [BUSINESS TYPE] Location: [CITY] Target Audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE] Main Services: [LIST OF SERVICES] Tone: [FRIENDLY / PROFESSIONAL / MODERN / TRUSTWORTHY] The website copy must include: 1. Homepage headline 2. Homepage subheadline 3. Intro paragraph 4. Services section 5. Why choose us section 6. Call-to-action section 7. Contact / booking section Requirements: - Keep language simple and easy to understand - Focus on customer benefits, not just features - Mention the location naturally - Avoid generic AI-sounding phrases - Add trust-building words - Write in a way that encourages bookings

5. Homepage Copy Prompt

Write homepage copy for a beauty salon named Glow & Grace Beauty Salon in Vijayawada. Target audience: Women aged 18–45 who want affordable, stylish, and professional beauty services. Tone: Friendly, elegant, welcoming, and modern. Include: - Headline - Subheadline - Short introductory paragraph - One short section explaining why this salon is different

7. Services Page Prompt

Write website service descriptions for Glow & Grace Beauty Salon in Vijayawada. Create content for these services: - Hair Styling - Bridal Makeup - Facials & Skin Care - Hair Spa - Manicure & Pedicure For each service include: - Service title - Short description - What is included - Why customers should choose this service

8. Services Page Content

1. Hair Styling

Description: Get a fresh new look with stylish haircuts, hair smoothing, straightening, curling, and party hairstyles.

What’s Included: Hair consultation, haircut and styling, blow dry, straightening or curling options, styling suggestions based on face shape.

Why Choose This Service: Our stylists help you choose a look that suits your personality and lifestyle.

2. Bridal Makeup

Description: Look stunning on your special day with our complete bridal makeup and styling package.

What’s Included: Bridal makeup, hairstyling, saree draping, trial session, long-lasting premium products.

Why Choose This Service: Our bridal experts create a look that matches your outfit, skin tone, and style.

3. Facials & Skin Care

Description: Refresh your skin with professional facials and skin treatments designed for different skin types.

What’s Included: Skin analysis, deep cleansing, facial massage, face mask, skin care suggestions.

Why Choose This Service: We use high-quality products that help your skin look fresh and glowing.

4. Hair Spa

Description: Repair damaged hair and relax your mind with our nourishing hair spa treatments.

What’s Included: Hair wash, hair mask, head massage, conditioning, blow dry.

Why Choose This Service: This service helps bring back softness, shine, and strength to your hair.

5. Manicure & Pedicure

Description: Give your hands and feet the care they deserve with our manicure and pedicure services.

What’s Included: Nail cleaning, scrubbing, massage, nail shaping, moisturizing treatment, nail polish.

Why Choose This Service: These treatments help you feel refreshed while keeping your nails neat and beautiful.

9. CTA Prompt

Write 5 strong call-to-action sections for Glow & Grace Beauty Salon. The CTAs should: - Encourage people to book an appointment - Build trust - Mention Vijayawada naturally - Include urgency or emotional appeal - Sound friendly and welcoming

10. CTA Sections

  • Ready for a New Look? Book your appointment today and let Glow & Grace help you feel confident and refreshed.
  • Getting Ready for a Wedding or Special Event? Our bridal and party makeup slots fill quickly, especially on weekends. Reserve your session today.
  • Visit the Salon Women in Vijayawada Trust Experience personalized beauty care in a clean, comfortable, and welcoming environment.
  • You Deserve Some Self-Care Take a break from your busy schedule and enjoy a relaxing beauty session designed just for you.
  • Not Sure Which Service is Right for You? Talk to our team and get a free consultation before booking.
